Frequently Asked Questions
How do I start a new escape room?

Go to your Task List, select the escape room you want to play, and click “Start Game”.

How can I view my achievements?

Click on the “Achievements” tab in the top navigation to see all badges and points you have earned.

Why isn’t the text-to-speech working?

Please make sure your browser supports Speech Synthesis, and that you have not muted it. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser.

Who can I contact for more help?

If you experience technical issues, please submit the form below or email us at escaperoom-support@unsw.edu.au.
